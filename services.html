Our Services

Professional textile machinery services with 35+ years of expertise

New Machine Jala Installation

We provide complete jala installation services for new weaving machines, ensuring perfect alignment, smooth performance, and long-lasting durability. Each jala is precisely designed to meet machine specifications and deliver maximum efficiency from day one.

Jala Replacement Service

When your existing jala shows signs of wear or damage, Texna offers quick and reliable jala replacement solutions. Our expert team ensures minimal machine downtime while upgrading your system with high-quality jala for improved productivity and weaving quality.

Jala Repair & Maintenance

Our jala repair services help restore your system's performance without the need for full replacement. We inspect, repair, and reinforce damaged jala components to extend their life and maintain consistent fabric output.

Customized Harness Solutions

We design and manufacture custom harness systems tailored to your specific machine type and fabric requirements. From design to delivery, our focus is on providing durability, precision, and performance you can rely on.
